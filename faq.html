FAQs
1Who can sell on mSupply?

Anyone who is a registered supplier with mSupply.com can sell their products on mSupply.

2How do I sell on mSupply?

Fill up the registration form and submit the documents. Once your documents are verified you are ready to sell on mSupply.com as a registered supplier.

3What are the documents required to register as a supplier on mSupply?
In order to sell on mSupply.com, you need to submit
  • A valid TIN/VAT number
  • PAN number and
  • Address proof
4I do not have a TIN, can I still register as a supplier?

No. TIN/VAT is mandatory to register.

5Will I get charged for listing products on mSupply?

No. Listing of products is free.

6Who takes care of the delivery of my products?

The suppliers have to take care of the delivery of products themselves. In certain pre-decided cases a third party logistic facility may be used.

7When can I start selling?

As soon as your registration is complete and your documents verified, you are ready to sell.

8How many listings are required to start selling?

You can start with as low as one product listing.

PRICING AND PAYMENTS
1Who decides the price of the product?

As a verified supplier, you have complete control on the price of the products.

2What is the fee charged as commission?

Commission fee varies from product to product. Suppliers would be notified about the commission charges at the time of registration itself.

3Please give an example to show the price calculation of a product.
MRP 1000
Offer Price* 860
mSupply Commission (5%) 43
VAT@14.5% 130.9
Net Price 1030.9

* Offer price is the price of the product that is agreed between the seller and mSupply.com for that particular product

4How and when do I get paid?

You will get paid once the goods are delivered and the invoice is verified. This can take a maximum of 5 working days post-delivery.

ORDER MANAGEMENT AND SHIPPING
1What products can I sell on mSupply?

You can sell any product based on the categories listed on our website. If your category is not listed, email us at support@msupply.com and we will guide you in listing the category if relevant to the business.

2Who takes care of the delivery of my products?

The suppliers have to take care of the delivery of products themselves. In certain pre-decided cases a third party logistic facility may be used.

3How do I manage my orders?

You can login to the Supplier App and navigate to Orders Page. In case of any confusion please reach out to our team. We would be more than happy to help you.

4Would I get compensation if the goods are damaged or lost in transit by mSupply logistics partner?

You would be notified of the terms & conditions of shipping through our logistic partners. We recommend you to go through that document for clarity.
